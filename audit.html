KPI Cascade Document Standard

1Every C-suite domain creates _KPI-CASCADE.md in their TEAMS directory. This is the single source of truth for all role KPIs in that domain.
2Role Archetypes defined per department. Each role assigned to exactly one archetype. Suggested archetypes: Revenue Driver, Operational, Support, Leadership, Specialist. Departments may define their own but must have 3–5 archetypes.
3Strict 4–5 KPI areas per role. No role may have fewer than 4 or more than 5. All weights must sum to exactly 100%.
4Measures capped by level: L1–L2 (IC) = max 3 measures per KPI area. L3–L4 (TL/Senior) = max 4. L5 (Exec/Head) = max 5.
5Cascade Tree: Visual hierarchy showing every role, reporting line, and archetype assignment. ASCII or Mermaid format.
6Verification Checklist: Minimum 8 rules validated — weights sum, CSAT present on client-facing roles, measures capped, outcome KPIs weighted highest, STI section present, Values Assessment present.
7Change Log: Every modification recorded with date, author, change description, and affected roles. No informal edits.
8Every role file must include: KPI table with weights · STI formula · Values Assessment (Keystones) · CSAT questions (if applicable) · Performance-Culture Matrix · Career progression.
